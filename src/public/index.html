DDoS Protection & Anycast Network

At Orbital, Inc., we elevate DDoS defense to the next level with our state-of-the-art Layer 4 and Layer 7 DDoS protection, utilizing cutting-edge technologies like XDP (eXpress Data Path) and VPP (Vector Packet Processing) for our firewall and anti-DDoS solutions. Our robust security framework includes stateful L4 filtering and L7 application filters, catering to a wide range of applications such as OpenVPN, HTTP, and more, with SYNProxy coming soon. We offer unparalleled customization for application filters through our Web Application Firewall (WAF), ensuring tailored security that meets your specific needs. All our servers, whether dedicated or VPS, include DDoS protection at no extra cost. Additionally, we provide remote DDoS protection via GRE and L4/7 proxy, safeguarding your infrastructure with our advanced anycast network for unmatched security and performance.

Virtual Machines & Dedicated Machines

Orbital, Inc. offers secure virtual and dedicated machines with built-in DDoS protection, ensuring your infrastructure is safeguarded from malicious attacks. Our virtual machines are hosted on powerful servers with high-speed SSDs, providing exceptional performance and reliability. Our dedicated machines are equipped with the latest hardware and are ideal for resource-intensive applications, providing the flexibility and control you need to manage your infrastructure. Both our virtual and dedicated machines are backed by our anycast network, ensuring unparalleled security and performance.
