Privacy Policy

Orbital, Inc., also known as "Orbital," values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal and data privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you engage with our services.

1. Processing of Personal Data

1.1 Orbital, Inc. may process your personal data when you utilize our services, including dedicated machines, virtual machines (VPS), and Layer 4/7 proxy/CDN/WAF/GRE services. This data is provided by you directly, for instance, when you contact us for information, apply for a position, or enter into a business relationship with us. We process this data to deliver our services and keep you informed. Without this data, we cannot provide our services or offer employment.

1.2 We may also receive information about you from third parties, ensuring that they have legally obtained and we have the right to use this information.

1.3 Additionally, we collect data on website usage to understand visitor behavior, such as visitor numbers, geographic data, and page views, using your IP address and domain name. This helps us tailor our website to user preferences.

1.4 Google Analytics, a service by Google Inc., is used on our website to monitor and analyze how visitors use our site. Google may share this data with third parties if required by law or if these third parties process the information on Google's behalf. We have no control over Google's data handling practices.

2. Use of Personal Data

2.1 Orbital, Inc. uses your data to provide requested information and services and to manage our website effectively. We may also use your information to update you on our services and conduct research to enhance our offerings.

2.2 We process your data based on legal grounds such as contract performance, legal obligations, or your consent, which you may withdraw at any time.

2.3 Our employees and third-party service providers are obligated to treat your information confidentially and securely.

3. Transfer to Third Parties

3.1 Orbital, Inc. does not sell or lease customer data. Your information will only be disclosed as required by law or to protect Orbital, Inc.'s rights and property or ensure the safety of our users or the public.

3.2 In some instances, third parties may process your data on our behalf, under strict confidentiality and privacy obligations.

4. Storage and Security of Personal Data

4.1 We store personal data no longer than necessary, except where law mandates longer storage. Orbital, Inc. employs appropriate security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

5.1 You have rights to access, rectify, delete, limit processing, or request data portability. To exercise these rights, please contact us at legal@orbitalsolutions.ca. Include a copy of your ID with sensitive information obscured for identity verification.
