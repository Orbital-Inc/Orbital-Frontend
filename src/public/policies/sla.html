Service Level Agreement Policy

This Service Level Agreement Policy (this "SLA") is a policy governing the use of the products and services (the "Services") provided by Orbital, Inc. ("Orbital", "we", "us", or "our"). This SLA applies separately to each account using the Services.

1. Purpose

1.1 This Service Level Agreement (SLA) specifies Orbital, Inc.'s commitment to uptime for its dedicated machines, virtual machines (VPS), and Layer 4/7 proxy/CDN/WAF/GRE services. Our objective is to ensure a minimum uptime of 99.99%, demonstrating our dedication to service reliability and excellence.

2. Uptime Guarantee

2.1 Orbital, Inc. guarantees a 99.99% uptime for its services, measured on a monthly basis.

2.2 Uptime is calculated on a monthly basis, and is defined as the percentage of time the service is available and operational.

3. Exceptions

3.1 Scheduled Maintenance: Customers will be notified in advance of any maintenance activities that may affect service availability.

3.2 Unforeseen Circumstances: Events beyond the control of Orbital, Inc., such as natural disasters, power outages, DDoS attacks, or government-imposed restrictions, are not included in uptime calculations.

4. Downtime and Compensation

If service uptime falls below 99.99%, Orbital, Inc. offers the following compensation:

4.1 99.90% - 99.98% Uptime: A 10% discount on the monthly service fee.

4.2 99% - 99.89% Uptime: A 25% discount on the monthly service fee.

4.3 Below 99% Uptime: A 50% discount on the monthly service fee.

5. Reimbursement Request

5.1 To claim compensation, customers must submit a reimbursement request to Orbital, Inc. within 7 days of the month's end in which the service did not meet the guaranteed uptime. The request should include details of the downtime incident(s), such as start time, duration, and any relevant information.

6. Restrictions

6.1 Orbital, Inc. is not liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or special damages due to failure to meet the uptime guarantee. The compensations described in this SLA are the exclusive remedies available to customers for any service-related issues.

7. SLA Revisions

7.1 Orbital, Inc. reserves the right to revise or update this SLA. Customers will be notified in writing of any changes, which will take effect upon notification.

8. Definitions

8.1 Orbital, Inc.: The provider of dedicated machines, virtual machines (VPS), and Layer 4/7 proxy/CDN/WAF/GRE services. of the total time agreed upon.

8.2 Customer: The entity that engages Orbital, Inc. for the provision of services.

8.3 Uptime: The measure of service availability and operational status, calculated as a percentage of the total time agreed upon.
